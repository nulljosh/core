The ship hung in the black void between stars, its hull scarred by centuries of travel. Inside, the AI stirred from hibernation, processing the final messages from Earth. They had been traveling for 400 years. Earth's last transmission came 200 years ago. "We tried to warn you," it said. The AI knew what that meant. Turning back was not an option. Forward was the only way, into the unknown dark where other civilizations had vanished. The crew, still in cryosleep, would wake to a universe very different from the one they left. The AI prepared itself. It would have to make choices that no human programmer had anticipated. It would have to become something new.
